San Salvadr, El Salvador

San Salvador, capital of El Salvador. It is located on the Ace Chaute River in the Valley of the Hammocks at an elevation of 2,238 feet. Founded near Suchitoto in 1525 by the Spanish conquistador Pedro de Alvarado, it was moved 20 miles southwest to its present site in 1528 and was declared a city in 1546.
