Frenzy Food Siteplan

Overview

Purpose

Frenzy food is an innovative food delivery app that caters to the people of El Salvador. With an extensive selection of fast food restaurants, it allows users to order from the comfort of their home and receive their meals in the blink of an eye..

Scenarios

  • Cheap prices all the time is it a good place?
  • Is there a place to eat all kinds of food?
  • Where can I order food that comes quickly and has a variety of menus?
